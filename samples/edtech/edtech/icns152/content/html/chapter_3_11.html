1. Introduction to Southeast Asia, ASEAN, and ASEAN’s forest cover

1.1 SE Asia

The SE Asia is a sub-region in Asia that is geographically south of China, east of India and north of Australia. This sub-region can be further categorized into the Asian mainland, and the island arcs and archipelagoes to the east and southeast. The former comprise Cambodia, Lao, Myanmar, Thailand and Viet Nam, and the latter comprise Brunei, Timor-Leste, Indonesia, Malaysia, the Philippines and Singapore.
