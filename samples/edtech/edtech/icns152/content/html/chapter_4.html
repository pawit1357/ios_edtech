Conclusion

The e-Learning instructional materials allow users to access, review, and revise the materials at their convenience from any location. Instead of being viewed as a replacement to traditional modes of teaching, the e-Learning instructional materials reinforce the course’s teaching and learning. In addition, preliminary observations and opportunistic qualitative assessment indicated that registered users have much benefitted from the e-Learning instructional materials for the ICNS152 Southeast Asia Ecology course, and the instructional materials have indeed reinforced the users’ learning and understanding of the course.
